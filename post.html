Documents Required to apply Business Loans for Women

Article updated on 8th January 2023

“Nobody talks about entrepreneurship as a survival, but that’s exactly what it is and what nurtures creative thinking. Running that first shop taught me business is not financial science; it’s about trading: buying and selling.”
—Anita Roddick, founder of The Body Shop
  1. Filled and duly signed Application Form .
  2. KYC Documents like Business entity proof, partnership deed, incorporation certificate, shops and establishment certificate, Articles of Association (AOA)
  3. Financial Details - Projected turnover and current year performance Tax audit reports, balance sheet, profit and loss report, VAT returns, audited and provisional financials, etc.
  4. The Bank statements of the applicants of the last 6 months must also be provided.
  5. Passport - size photograph of the applicant(s) should also be submitted.
  6. The PAN Card of partners, directors, proprietors, promoters and the business entity
  7. Address Proof of the business entity and the stakeholders.
